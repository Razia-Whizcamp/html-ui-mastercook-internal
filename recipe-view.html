Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, traditionally known as murga makhani, is an Indian dish originating in Delhi. It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter(makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture.

Serving ideas

Butter chicken is a popular Indian dish made with chicken, spices, tomatoes & cream. This creamy & authentic tasting butter chicken is the best you can make at home. Serve butter chicken with Turmeric rice, Cumin rice, basmati rice or naan.

Ingredients

Edit
  • 1 cup butter, divided

  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken
